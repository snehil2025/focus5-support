Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your Focus5 account, you can do so directly within the app. Here's how:

  1. Open the Focus5 app on your device
  2. Navigate to Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  3. Tap Confirm to permanently delete your account and all associated data

If you face any issues, feel free to contact us at support@focus5.com.
